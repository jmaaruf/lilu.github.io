How You Stole My Heart

When we went out on our first date
I didn’t have many expectations.

I had been on a few first dates before,
but you surprised me.

I saw something in you that I couldn’t even explain.
You were like gold in a world full of dirt,
and I immediately thought to myself:
“This girl is different… and I want her.”

As we kept going on more dates,
the more I liked you.

I was so happy the day we put a label on our relationship.
I was so proud to have a beautiful girl like you
to call my girlfriend.

Then I was told I would go to Hawassa for the next two months.
I was devastated —
I thought there was no way you would be open to trying long distance,
and I was scared to lose you.

But you surprised me once again
by telling me you were willing to try.

On our last date before I left for Hawassa,
I wanted to say a proper goodbye to you
and finally kiss you —
something I had wanted to do since our first date.

But I was still so nervous.
I thought it might be too much, too fast,
that you would stop wanting to see me.

So I ended up not doing it…
and I regretted it deeply
because I would have to wait weeks to see you again.

When you later told me
that you don’t kiss guys until you get married,
I was so sad.

I had been thinking about kissing you since our first date.
I wanted to share that intimate moment with you as soon as I could.

But I understood…
and I began to think how much more special it would be
if we waited until then.

Even now, every time I see you,
I still think about kissing you.
I have imagined it a thousand times
and every time it was exactly everything I wanted it to be.

After I went to Hawassa,
we started having communication problems —
mainly on my part.

I realised I was hurting you,
and that started to hurt me too.

I wanted to do better.
I tried my best…
but this was not the type of person I had been before I met you.
It took time,
and I hurt you many times.

I hated myself for putting you through all of that.
You deserved none of it.

But you still stuck by me.

That was the moment I started to realise:
I may be in love with this girl…
because I had never felt this way toward anyone before.

The feeling only grew stronger from that point on.
You started to look more beautiful every time I saw you.
I still felt nervous every time I saw you.
My heart beat faster,
and I was so scared of doing the wrong thing
and ruining our relationship.

I loved you because you are so beautiful.
I never get tired of looking at your gorgeous face
and your sexy all-around body.

You are so loving and caring.
You showed me what real love feels like.
You are the best thing that has ever happened to me.

I’m not very good at expressing how I feel toward someone —
I have been like that all my life.

But I mean this with every part of me:
I love you more than you will ever know…
and I will love you more every single day.

Cherished Memories

I will never forget the day we first went to the movies.
I have never felt that close to you.

I was thinking about you the whole time —
I don’t even remember what the movie was about.

You holding on to my arms like that,
you kissing me…
it was just the perfect date for me.

That type of closeness with you meant so much.
I was wishing the movie never ended.

That was also the first day I kissed you
and I was so happy.
I wanted to do it again and again.

I thought that would be the best date we would ever have,
then we started having dates in the car
and that felt even better.

Holding on to you while we talked was the best feeling.
I felt more connected to you
and it was just us there —
no other distractions,
and I could give all of me to you in there.

Even though we did nothing,
those were the best dates for me.
I could do it every day without ever getting tired of it.

The little moments we have —
like holding hands when we walk,
you kissing me randomly when we are talking,
and you playing with my hands —
are the best.

I get excited every time
and I could never get enough of them.

I am grateful for the times we got to spend together till now
and I hope there are better times in the future as well.
